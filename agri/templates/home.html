Nutrient Analysis

Soil testing helps determine the levels of essential nutrients such as nitrogen, phosphorus, potassium, and micronutrients (like zinc, iron, etc.) present in the soil. This information is vital for optimizing fertilizer application to ensure crops receive adequate nutrition.

pH Level

Soil pH affects nutrient availability to plants. Testing reveals whether the soil is acidic, neutral, or alkaline, which guides decisions on soil amendments (like lime) to adjust pH levels for optimal plant growth.

Organic Matter Content

Soil testing measures the amount of organic matter present. Organic matter improves soil structure, water retention, and nutrient availability, making it a key factor in soil fertility.
